About Me

Vittorio is an entrepreneur and innovator in the photography and franchising industry. His extensive qualifications and experience include being a Grand Master of Photography with the Australian Institute of Professional Photography (GM.Photog), and Past President of the Australian institute of Professional Photography. Vittorio has been in the wedding photography industry for over 40 years and has won numerous accolades for his photography including AIPP Wedding Photographer of the Year. He brings his extensive industry experience to the digital age with Emot.
